Fabio is Professor of Language and Knowledge Technologies at the University of Sheffield. His research focuses on methodologies and techniques for Human Language Technologies, Machine Learning and Semantic Web technologies for Knowledge Management and for Web applications.

  1. •He is the director of the EU-funded X-Media, a 15 partner, €13.6M ($18.2M) integrated project on large scale knowledge management across multiple media (http://www.x-media-project.org). Other active projects:

  2. •EPSRC1-funded IRC project Advanced Knowledge technologies (AKT), a £7.5M ($14.8M), 5 university project on advanced knowledge technologies; role: co-investigator and technical manager for the University of Sheffield. 

  3. •IPAS, a project jointly funded by the Department of Trade and Industry and Rolls Royce plc (http://www.3worlds.org/) on the application of intelligent technologies for the integration of design and product services for jet engines; role: principal investigator.

  4. •AHRC-EPSRC jointly funded project eArchaeology on mining and extraction of information from over 1M web documents. Role: Principal investigator. 

  5. •AHRC-funded project: "ARMADILLO: Information Mining in Distributive Research Datasets in the Arts and Humanities" (http://www.hrionline.ac.uk/armadillo/). Role: principal investigator.

  6. •EPSRC funded project Abraxas on learning ontologies from documents (http://nlp.shef.ac.uk/abraxas/); role co-investigator.

  7. •Horizon Scanner project funded by the Environment Agency (http://nlp.shef.ac.uk/wig/projects/environment-agency/index.html); role: principal investigator.

  8. •European project MultiMatch on applying cross-media technologies to cultural heritages (http://www.multimatch.org/): role co-investigator.

  9. •the project “Management of Personal Digital Pictures” funded by Kodak Eastman; role: principal investigator.


In the period 2002-2005, he was project director of Dot.Kom, a project funded by the European Commission on the use of Adaptive Information Extraction for Knowledge Management.

He has open collaborations with many companies and organizations such as Rolls Royce plc, Kodak, Centro Ricerche Fiat, Quinary, the UK Environmental Agency, etc.

In the period 1993-2000 Fabio was researcher at ITC-IRST in Trento Italy.

From 1988 until 1993 he was researcher at Centro Ricerche Fiat in Orbassano, Torino, Italy. 

Fabio is member of the editorial board of the international journal on Web Semantics: Science, Services and Agents on the World Wide Web and of the International Journal of Human Computer Studies. 

Fabio holds a PhD from the University of East Anglia, UK, and an MSc in

Information Sciences from the University of Torino, Italy.
