ML4IE Paper

Fabio Ciravegna
"Learning to Tag for Information Extraction from Text"
in Fabio Ciravegna, Roberto Basili, Robert Gaizauskas (eds.) ECAI Workshop on Machine Learning for Information Extraction, workshop in conjunction with ECAI2000, Berlin, August 2000.

Abstract

LearningPinocchio is an algorithm for adaptive information extraction. It learns template filling rules that insert SGML tags into texts. LearningPinocchio is based on a covering algorithm that learns rules by bottom-up generalization of instances in a tagged corpus. It has been tested on three scenarios in informal domains in two languages (Italian and English). Experiments report excellent results with respect to the current state of the art.
